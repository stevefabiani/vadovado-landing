One trip, all of it.

Six categories. One rollup. Costs you actually understand — not a stack of receipts after you get home.

ITINERARY

Days, not chaos

Build the trip a day at a time. Hotels, flights, trains, activities, meals — all in the order you’ll actually do them.

COSTS

Six-category rollup

Transportation, lodging, activities, meals, misc — and a budget if you want one. Spent, remaining, and a Quick Facts dashboard that tells you what each night really cost.

MAP

See the trip

Every hotel, every POI, every transport leg on a real map. Plan the routing before you’re standing on a corner with a roller bag.

PRIVATE

Your vault, your file

The whole trip lives in a single encrypted .vado file. iCloud Drive, Files, AirDrop — wherever you already keep things. No account required.

NATIVE

iOS + Mac, one bundle

Same vault, same code, two platforms. Edit on the train, finish on the laptop. Works fully offline.

SECURE

Two tiers, your call

Basic locks the file with a baked-in app key. Advanced adds a passphrase + 12-word recovery phrase you control end-to-end.

Local-first. File-based. Yours.

Most apps want your data so they can keep you. VadoVado wants you because the app does the job. Your trip lives in a single file you can read, move, back up, or delete — without us, without a server, without a sign-in. The way software used to work, with the polish of how it should.
